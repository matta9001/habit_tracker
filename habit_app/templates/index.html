📈 HabitStake

The things we want in life may seem like an immense effort away. In reality, we usually just need to put in a little effort consistently over long focused periods.

But if we're not careful and lose focus, time will pass us by.

This app creates a ritual to sustain long term focus, with financial and social stakes on the line to help us stay accountable.

Every new account starts with a free streak, spending money isn't required to get started.

If you lose that, you can purchase a streak, creating some more pressure to stay focused.

It also helps fund websites like these, which aren't free.

No software application is a substitute for discipline.

This app is designed as a tool for those who are independently motivated to work on a long term goal.

Sharing your streak publicly means more people will see if you didn't stay consistent. Money puts tangible value on a commitment to goals.

Leveraging these accountabilities will only make us motivated to be the best version of ourselves.
